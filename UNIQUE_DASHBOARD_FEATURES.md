# Unique Dashboard Features - Not Already on Website

## 🎯 **Innovative Features to Add**

Based on typical dashboard implementations, here are unique features that likely don't exist yet:

---

## 💡 **1. AI-Powered Research Recommendations**

**What it is**: Intelligent system that suggests research collaborations

**Components**:
- **"Find Your Research Partner" Tool**
  - Input: Researcher's current work/SDG focus
  - Output: Top 5 recommended collaborators
  - Shows: Compatibility score, shared interests, complementary expertise
- **Visualization**: Interactive recommendation cards with connection lines
- **Unique Value**: Goes beyond simple search - uses AI to match researchers

**Implementation**:
- Analyze keyword overlap
- Calculate SDG alignment scores
- Identify complementary research areas
- Show potential collaboration benefits

---

## 📊 **2. Research Impact Score Dashboard**

**What it is**: Quantified impact metrics for each publication/researcher

**Components**:
- **Impact Score Calculator**
  - Factors: Citations, SDG alignment, journal tier, real-world applications
  - Score: 0-100 scale
  - Trend: Impact score over time
- **Leaderboard**: Top researchers by impact score
- **Visualization**: Heatmap showing impact scores by department/SDG
- **Unique Value**: Provides measurable impact beyond just publication count

**Metrics to Include**:
- Citation count (if available)
- Journal tier (Financial Times, UT Dallas flags)
- SDG coverage breadth
- Recent publications (recency bonus)
- Interdisciplinary collaboration

---

## 🔮 **3. Predictive Research Trends Dashboard**

**What it is**: Forecasts future research directions

**Components**:
- **Trend Prediction Model**
  - Analyzes historical patterns
  - Predicts: Which SDGs will grow, which departments will expand
  - Shows: Confidence intervals, scenario planning
- **Visualization**: 
  - Forecast lines on trend charts
  - "What-if" scenarios
  - Emerging topic detection
- **Unique Value**: Helps leadership plan strategically

**Features**:
- 5-year forecast for each SDG
- Department growth predictions
- Emerging keyword trends
- Resource allocation recommendations

---

## 🎯 **4. SDG Gap Analysis Tool**

**What it is**: Identifies research gaps and opportunities

**Components**:
- **Gap Analysis Matrix**
  - Shows: Which SDGs are under-researched
  - Compares: Your coverage vs. global priorities
  - Recommends: Areas for investment
- **Visualization**: 
  - Heatmap showing gaps (red = low coverage, green = high)
  - Opportunity score for each SDG
  - Comparison with peer institutions (if data available)
- **Unique Value**: Strategic planning tool for leadership

**Output**:
- "SDG 6 (Water) has only 4 publications - opportunity for growth"
- "SDG 3 (Health) is strong - consider expanding"
- "Recommended focus areas for next 2 years"

---

## 🤝 **5. Collaboration Network Explorer**

**What it is**: Advanced network analysis beyond simple connections

**Components**:
- **Interactive Network Graph**
  - Nodes: Researchers, Departments, SDGs, External Partners
  - Edges: Collaboration strength, shared publications, co-authorship
  - Filters: By SDG, department, time period
- **Network Metrics**:
  - Centrality scores (who are the key connectors?)
  - Clustering analysis (which groups collaborate most?)
  - Bridge detection (who connects different groups?)
- **Unique Value**: Reveals hidden collaboration patterns

**Advanced Features**:
- Click node to see all connections
- Highlight shortest path between researchers
- Identify isolated researchers (collaboration opportunities)
- Show collaboration strength over time

---

## 📈 **6. Real-Time Research Activity Feed**

**What it is**: Live updates on research activity

**Components**:
- **Activity Stream**
  - New publications added
  - SDG coverage updates
  - Department milestones
  - Recent collaborations
- **Notifications**: 
  - "New publication in SDG 3"
  - "Business Administration reached 250 sustainable publications"
  - "New collaboration detected between Finance and Accountancy"
- **Visualization**: Timeline view, activity heatmap
- **Unique Value**: Keeps stakeholders engaged with live updates

**Features**:
- Real-time or near-real-time updates
- Filterable by department, SDG, researcher
- Shareable milestones
- Achievement badges

---

## 🗺️ **7. Research Journey Map**

**What it is**: Visualizes research lifecycle and impact pathway

**Components**:
- **Journey Visualization**
  - Start: Research idea/keyword
  - Middle: Publication, SDG alignment
  - End: Real-world impact, applications
- **Path Analysis**:
  - Most common research paths
  - Time from idea to publication
  - Impact trajectory
- **Visualization**: Sankey diagram or flow chart
- **Unique Value**: Shows research evolution, not just outcomes

**Stages to Show**:
1. Research Initiation (keywords, topics)
2. Publication (journal, year)
3. SDG Alignment
4. Impact (citations, applications)
5. Future Directions

  - Save custom views
- **Widget Library**:

- Researcher: Personal research dashboard

- Donor: Impact-focused view
